MBA Admissions

MBA Mock Interview

A realistic Personal Interview (PI) simulation for MBA admissions, evaluating communication, confidence, and clarity of thought.

What you get:
  • 30–40 minute realistic interview simulation
  • Questions based on your profile, academics, and experiences
  • HR, opinion-based, and situation-based question practice
  • Feedback on communication, answer structure, and confidence
  • Suggestions to improve interview approach and answer framing
Ideal for:

Students preparing for MBA admission interviews and selection processes.

Campus Placement

Campus Placement Mock Interview

Structured placement interview practice for Chemical Engineering students targeting core industries — Oil & Gas, Petrochemicals, Process, and Manufacturing.

What you get:
  • 20–30 minute simulated campus interview
  • Technical and HR interview question practice
  • Feedback on technical clarity, explanation skills, and confidence
  • Guidance on structured answer delivery
Ideal for:

Chemical Engineering students preparing for core company campus placements.

Group Skills

GD / GE Session

Guided practice for Group Discussion and Group Exercise rounds — helping candidates communicate ideas, collaborate, and lead effectively.

What you get:
  • Live GD/GE practice session
  • Topic-based group discussion
  • Feedback on communication, participation, and clarity
  • Tips to improve structure, engagement, and impact
Skills evaluated:
  • Communication and articulation
  • Logical thinking and listening ability
  • Leadership and teamwork

Wellbeing

Stress Management Session

A supportive space to discuss academic and career stress — with practical techniques to improve focus, emotional balance, and mental clarity.

What you learn:
  • Common causes of stress and pressure
  • Techniques to manage anxiety and overthinking
  • Methods to improve focus and emotional balance
  • Practical strategies for challenging situations

Not a medical or psychological treatment program. For serious mental health concerns, please consult a qualified professional.
